With a background as both a cabinetmaker and furniture designer Jonas Lyndby Jensen has a broad understanding of both craft and design, especially within wooden furniture based on the Scandinavian tradition. Since his graduation from The Danish Design School he has developed, prototyped and exhibited furniture design cooperating with various design manufacturers in Denmark and internationally.

With respect for the Nordic heritage and furniture classics, Jonas attempts to develop and renew furniture and use of materials, adapting modern technology and production methods.
